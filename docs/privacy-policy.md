# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Joritna**.

Joritna ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights when using the Joritna mobile application and related services.

By using Joritna, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

# 2. About Joritna

Joritna is a private residential community platform that enables communication between:

- Building Managers
- Tenants

The platform provides secure access to building-specific information, announcements, community chat, Share & Help, and profile management.

# 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Authenticate your account
- Maintain secure sessions
- Deliver push notifications
- Display your profile
- Enable community communication
- Manage buildings
- Improve application stability
- Respond to support requests
- Protect against abuse and unauthorized access

We do **not** sell your personal information.

# 5. Push Notifications

Joritna uses **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** to send notifications such as:

- New announcements
- Community chat activity
- Share & Help updates
- Building-related notifications

Notification tokens are stored securely and may be removed when they become inactive or invalid.

- Profile avatars

- Community attachments

- JWT access tokens
